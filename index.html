FAQ

Important questions customers will ask

Kya sab products ready stock me honge?

Nahi, store on-demand model par bana hai. Order aane ke baad sourcing ho sakti hai.

Delivery kitne din me hogi?

Normally sourcing aur shipping ke baad 3-7 business days target rakha gaya hai.

Customized items return ho sakte hain?

Personalized products usually non-returnable hote hain except damage or wrong item cases.

Payment kab live hoga?

Payment methods later add kiye ja sakte hain once store content and products are finalized.
